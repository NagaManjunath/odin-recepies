Lemon Rice

Description

When it comes to quick and delicious meals, lemon rice is a classic that never disappoints. This vibrant and zesty dish is perfect for busy worknight dinners when you crave something comforting yet effortless. Let's dive into how you can whip up a mouthwatering batch of lemon rice in just 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of cooked rice
  • 1 medium onion, chopped fine
  • 1/3 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • A pinch of hing
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds (rai)
  • 1 tsp chana dal
  • 1/2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • A handful of roasted peanuts
  • Juice of one lemon
  • Salt, to taste

Steps

Step 1 : Prepare The Base

Heat vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and chana dal, letting them sizzle until the mustard seeds begin to pop.

Step 2 : Add Spices And Onions

Stir in curry leaves, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and hing. Add the finely chopped onion and cook until golden brown.

Step 3 : Add Lemon And Rice

Squeeze the juice of a whole lemon into the pan. Add the cooked rice and mix gently to combine all ingredients.

Step 4 : Finish With Peanuts

Sprinkle a handful of roasted peanuts on top, giving the dish a crunchy texture.

Step 5 : Serve

Serve warm, and enjoy your quick and flavorful lemon rice.
